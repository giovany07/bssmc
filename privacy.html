Privacy Policy

How BSSMC handles information shared through this website

This Privacy Policy explains how information submitted through the BSSMC website may be collected, used, and protected.

BSSMC respects your privacy and values the protection of any personal information you may provide through this website.

This Privacy Policy explains the general manner in which information may be handled when users visit the website or submit inquiries.

1. Information We May Collect

When you use this website, BSSMC may receive information that you voluntarily provide, such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Address or other membership-related details
  • Messages or inquiries submitted through forms

2. How Information May Be Used

Information submitted through the website may be used for the following purposes:

  • Responding to inquiries and messages
  • Processing membership interest or service requests
  • Providing cooperative-related updates or communication
  • Improving website content and member support

3. Protection of Information

BSSMC takes reasonable steps to protect information shared through the website. However, no website or online platform can guarantee absolute security.

Users are encouraged not to submit highly sensitive personal information unless specifically required and officially requested by BSSMC.

4. Sharing of Information

BSSMC does not sell personal information to third parties. Information may only be shared when necessary for legitimate cooperative purposes, legal compliance, or authorized internal processing.

5. Third-Party Links

This website may include links to external platforms such as social media pages or other websites. Once you leave the BSSMC website, third-party privacy policies will apply.

BSSMC is not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.

6. Cookies and Website Data

Depending on the hosting platform or website tools being used, limited technical information such as browser type, device data, or site usage patterns may be collected automatically for website performance and analytics purposes.

7. User Rights and Requests

If you wish to inquire about information you submitted through the website, or if you want to request updates or corrections, you may contact BSSMC directly using the contact information provided on the website.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

BSSMC may revise this Privacy Policy as needed to reflect website updates, internal processes, or legal requirements. Updates may be posted on this page without prior notice.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how your information may be handled, please contact BSSMC through the Contact Us page.
