Our Story

Growing from local initiative into a cooperative with purpose

Bokod Sulfur Spring Multipurpose Cooperative (BSSMC) was established in [year] by dedicated community members who recognized the value and potential of the sulfur spring. What began as a simple initiative steadily grew into a cooperative that supports both members and the wider community.


Known for its therapeutic properties, the sulfur spring became the foundation of the cooperative’s identity. Over time, BSSMC expanded its work beyond tourism and into sulfur-based products, agricultural support, member-centered services, and community development programs.

Our Mission

To serve with sustainability and purpose

To provide sustainable livelihood opportunities and quality services to our members while preserving and promoting the natural heritage of the sulfur spring.

Our Vision

To become a stronger and more progressive cooperative

A self-reliant and progressive cooperative that enhances the quality of life of its members and contributes to meaningful community development.
